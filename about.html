💳 Bihar Student Credit Card (BSCC)

What is BSCC? A state government scheme providing interest-free loans to students for higher education.

  • Loan up to ₹4 lakhs
  • No interest during study period
  • Flexible repayment options

Eligibility: 10+2 pass, age 18-25, domicile of Bihar.

Documents: Aadhar, 10+2 marksheet, income certificate, bank account.
